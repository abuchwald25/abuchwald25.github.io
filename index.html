Welcome to queersources, your one-stop-shop for everything LGBTQ+ in the Boston area. Whether you’re looking for community, leisure, activity, help, health, we’re here to link you to the resources you need.

What is Queersources?

Queersources was born from a research project on the design of the LGBTQ+ liberation movement. During this research, we realized the lack of centralized resources for LGBTQ+ folks, as well as the lack of spaces that weren't focused solely on leisure and safety.
